Sience 4 Africa is a non-profit organization based in New York, U.S.A., empowering African scientists through capacity-building in research. The collaboration framework "S4AT" is exposed through this site, providing tools for researchers and contributors to collaborate and share ideas, lectures, grants and equipment across the world.
